Built for focus, not friction

Every feature in TaskManager maps to something real in the app — nothing is vaporware.

Task Management

Create, edit, and organize tasks with priority levels (Low / Medium / High), planned completion dates, and rich descriptions.

Subtask Kanban Board

Break any task into subtasks and track them through a full Kanban board: To Do → In Progress → In Review → Complete.

Calendar View

Visualize all your tasks on a monthly calendar. See what's due this week at a glance and plan your workload without surprises.

Analytics Dashboard

Track total tasks, completion rate, overdue count, and priority distribution — with live data pulled directly from your account.

Real-Time Notifications

Receive in-app notifications whenever tasks or subtasks are created, updated, deleted, or change status — plus persistent toast alerts.

Admin Panel

Admin accounts can view all users and tasks, update roles and usernames, and delete accounts — with a full management interface.
